# Solo Operator Opportunity Shapes

Catalog of six opportunity shapes for solo/1-3-person operators, with explicit capital floor, expertise floor, network requirement, time-to-revenue, gross margin, and scale ceiling. Attribution: [R-2026-05-17-k-shape-opportunities, 2026-05-17]

**Important preamble:** All BELIEF scores in this page are CONDITIONAL on the operator passing the 3-dimension edge-verification. See [[3-dimension-edge-verification-framework]] for the full check. If any of the three dimensions are absent, default to BLS-anchored ~25% 3-year-profit-positive rather than 42% for commit+edge. See also [[solo-entrepreneur-reference-class-forecasting]] for base rates.

## Shape A — AI Deployment Productized Service

**Capital floor:** $10K-$50K (tool stack, outbound, runway)
**Expertise floor:** Prior operational network in target vertical + AI toolchain proficiency (2-year learning curve for most)
**Network requirement:** 3-5 clients from prior domain work — REQUIRED, not optional
**Time-to-revenue:** $100-200K MRR at 12 months is realistic median
**Gross margin:** 60-80%
**Scale ceiling:** $200-500K ARR solo (15-25 clients, 2-4hrs/client/week cap)

BELIEF[viable-2026] = 0.72. BELIEF[viable-2026-2028] = 0.60 (drop 12pp per DA[#10] — B2B discretionary tech projects are asset-market-sensitive).

Shape A is **window-dependent**: strong in 2026, meaningful uncertainty 2027-2028. Unconditional (exploring-stage, no edge verified) BELIEF = ~0.55.

The moat is NOT the label "vertical AI specialist" — that is now consensus. The moat is prior customer relationships + specific implementation depth in a vertical (e.g., Yardi-API-level integration depth for property management).

Source: T2 independent research; cross-confirmed by tech-industry-analyst F[TIA-4] in R-2026-05-17. Saturation-cascade finding from DA[#6] downgraded from 0.72 to 0.65.

## Shape B — Micro-SaaS (Vertical-Specific Tool)

**Capital floor:** $10K-$50K
**Expertise floor:** Technical (coding or AI-codegen) + domain insight
**Network requirement:** None required — PLG distribution via integrations/communities/marketplaces viable
**Time-to-revenue:** 3-6 months first customer; 12-24 months to $10K MRR median
**Gross margin:** 70-90%
**Scale ceiling:** $100-500K ARR solo (AI-augmented upward revision)

**Calibration against optimistic narratives:**
- 87% of solo founders never break $10K MRR
- Median profitable micro-SaaS = $2.1K MRR
- "95% reach profitability year 1" is a definitional artifact — at $1/mo revenue, not replacement income

BELIEF[achieves $10K MRR within 24mo with pre-validated niche] = 0.55.

Shape B is the **most macro-agnostic** of the six shapes — recurring revenue is sticky in both directions once an ARR base is established. BELIEF[viable-2026-2028] essentially unchanged from BELIEF[viable-2026] (unlike window-dependent shapes).

Source: Rocking Web 2025 (T2), Freemius 2025 State of Micro-SaaS (T2), fn7.io 2025 (T2). CONVERGENT across independent sources.

## Shape C — Niche Agency (Specialized)

**Capital floor:** $10K-$30K
**Expertise floor:** Track record in specialty + client list
**Network requirement:** Industry contacts for first 3-5 clients
**Time-to-revenue:** 30-90 days first client
**Gross margin:** 40-75% (niche) vs 18-22% (generalist)
**Scale ceiling:** Owner earnings $200K-$500K at boutique scale

**Critical profitability inversion at scale:**
- Boutiques <10 FTE: 19% net margin
- 8-figure agencies: 8% net margin
- Owner-operator boutique $1-3M ARR with contractors out-earns $10-20M agency on per-hour owner economics

The structural incentive to scale is MISALIGNED with owner economics. Scaling from boutique to mid-size reduces per-owner-hour earnings. Optimal size for Shape C is boutique (<10 FTE), not scale.

Source: Predictable Profits 2025 Agency Benchmark (300+ agencies) — T1. VERIFIED.

## Shape D — Newsletter / Paid Community (Niche Authority)

**Capital floor:** $1-$10K
**Expertise floor:** Domain authority (existing)
**Network requirement:** Initial audience seeding required
**Time-to-revenue:** 6-18 months to $10-30K MRR (1,000 paid subscribers at $10-30/mo)
**Gross margin:** 80-95%
**Scale ceiling:** $200K-$1M ARR for strong niche operators

**DEMOTED from primary to ancillary shape for capital-deployable operator profiles** (per DA[#16] concede in R-2026-05-17):

For operators with capital and M&A deployment options (Shape E viable), Shape D pursued as PRIMARY creates an asymmetric downside:
- Expected financial loss if fails at month 18: $10-30K direct + $300-500K opportunity cost (at market rate for capital-deployable operator)
- Requires asset the operator may not have: existing audience authority + 18+ months of consistent content production
- Does NOT leverage the operator's distinctive assets (capital + domain network)

BELIEF[Shape D fails as primary for capital-deployable user] = 0.75.
BELIEF[viable as ancillary] = 0.65.

Shape D is VIABLE as an ancillary revenue layer stacked with Shape A or Shape F. Shape D as primary = terminal failure pattern for capital-deployable operator profiles.

Source: beehiiv/Ghost subscription metrics (T2).

## Shape E — Buy-and-Improve Micro-PE / ETA (Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition)

**Capital floor:** $50K-$300K liquid all-in; SBA 7(a) covers 80-90% of purchase price
**Target EBITDA:** $200K-$1.5M
**Deal multiples:** 4x-6x EBITDA (54% of independent sponsor deals, IBBA 2025)
**Time-to-revenue:** Immediate post-close; 12-18 months operational stability
**Scale ceiling:** Unlimited via bolt-on acquisitions

**RCA base rates (Stanford SF Study 2024 — T1):**
- Solo search IRR: 30.3%; 4.5x ROI
- 69.5% of acquired companies generate positive returns
- 11% achieve >10x
- 63% of searchers reach acquisition
- Median purchase in broader study: $14.4M @ 7.0x EBITDA; self-funded searchers target $500K-$3M enterprise value at 4-6x

BELIEF[viable for operator with prior industry + M&A process knowledge] = 0.65.
BELIEF[viable for pure financial buyer without industry background] = 0.30.
P(3yr-profit-positive | commit + verified-edge) = 42%.

Shape E is the **most macro-agnostic** of the six shapes. Acquiring a profitable SMB with established recurring revenue is least exposed to temporal K-shape inversion — the acquired business survives an equity correction if it has operational fundamentals. K-shape thesis is not required for the acquisition itself to generate returns.

Shape E is the **structurally superior terminal shape** for operators with capital + M&A process knowledge. Productized service (Shape A) is the better STARTING shape for operators without capital or M&A knowledge — these are sequential, not competing.

Source: Stanford GSB 2024 Search Fund Study Case E-870 (T1), IBBA Q2 2025 M&A Market Pulse (T2), GoSBA 2025 (T1). CONVERGENT.

## Shape F — AI Compliance / Red-Teaming Boutique

**Capital floor:** $10-50K (certification, liability insurance, tooling)
**Expertise floor:** AI governance + regulatory fluency (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF) — domain-credential moat
**Network requirement:** 1 anchor client connection critical
**Time-to-revenue:** 60-120 days
**Revenue potential:** $200+/hr senior practitioner rate; $300K-$800K/yr per enterprise client
**Gross margin:** 70-85%
**Scale ceiling:** 2-3 person team clears $1-2M ARR

**EU AI Act regulatory driver:**
- Fully applicable: August 2, 2026 (hard deadline)
- Fines: up to €30M or 6% global annual turnover (Article 99) for prohibited violations; €15M or 3% for high-risk systems
- Insurance companies requiring "AI Security Riders" with documented red-teaming evidence

BELIEF[demand timing] = 0.85 HIGH — regulatory mandate is not discretionary.
BELIEF[margin potential] = 0.60 MEDIUM — bill rate range unconfirmed (OQ[TIA-2] non-blocking carry-forward).

Shape F is **window-dependent**:
- BELIEF[viable-2026-H2] = 0.70
- BELIEF[viable-2026-2028 extended] = 0.55 (enforcement-intensity depends on regulatory agency priority)

Strong Q2 2026 through early 2027; more uncertain 2028.

Source: market.us (T2), Mend.io (T2); cross-confirmed by tech-industry-analyst F[TIA-3] in R-2026-05-17.

## Non-Catalog Shape: Distribution Arbitrage via Institutional Content Licensing

**Capital floor:** $50-200K (licensing, legal, data-prep)
**Network requirement:** Prior domain relationship network with access to non-public operational documentation — REQUIRED (not cold-start)
**Scale ceiling:** $500K-$2M depending on content uniqueness

Concept: acquiring or licensing deep-domain proprietary content (training manuals, court filings, clinical trial protocols) and productizing as fine-tuning datasets or RAG corpora for AI companies.

BELIEF[genuinely non-consensus vs. lagging consensus] = 0.50 — honest uncertainty. May be lagging consensus rather than pre-consensus; not in the Indie Hackers narrative but cannot confirm it isn't already being labeled elsewhere.

Carry-forward for user evaluation if prior domain relationships make this viable.
