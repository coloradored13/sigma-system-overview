### 1. Global Remittance Market Size and Growth
- Global remittances 2024: $905B total (+4.2% YoY); LMICs: $685B (+5.8% vs +1.2% in 2023)
- 2023 total: $865B; acceleration trend from post-COVID slowdown
- Remittances > FDI + ODA combined for developing countries (2023+2024)
- Top recipients by volume (2024e): India $129B, Mexico $68B, China $48B, Philippines $40B, Pakistan $33B
- Top recipients by GDP%: Tajikistan 45%, Tonga 38%, Nicaragua 27%, Lebanon 27%, Samoa 26%
- Regional growth 2024: South Asia +11.8%, LAC +5.5%, MENA +5.4%, ECA +3.0%, EAP ex-China +3.3%, SSA +2.4%
- US to LAC corridor: ~$170B (80% from US); Mexico expects ~2% growth 2025

- Informal channels believed to add 20-40% above recorded flows

### 2. Remittance Pricing Trends
- Global avg cost to send $200: 6.49% (Q1 2025); RPW database index: 6.21%
- Digital remittance avg: 5.06%; non-digital: ~7%
- By provider type ($200): Banks ~12.66%, MTOs ~5.35%, Mobile carriers ~3.87%
- Cheapest funding: debit/credit card 4.40%; cheapest disbursement: debit card 3.80%
- FX markup = ~35% of total cost; in some EM corridors up to 80%
- Sub-Saharan Africa: 8.2% avg (Q1 2025), down from 9.8% Q2 2016; East Africa 9.9%, Southern Africa 8.9%, West Africa 5.9%
- Most expensive corridor: Tanzania to Uganda at 33.58% for $500 transfer
- LAC unweighted avg: 3.67%; weighted avg: 3%; effective for $300+: below 2.4%
- SDG 10.c target (3% by 2030): Africa will NOT meet at current trajectory; LAC closer but uneven
- Digital vs cash: digital ~2pp cheaper globally but non-digital sometimes faster
- 4 consecutive years of pricing stability (William Blair)

### 4. Economics of Stablecoin-Based Remittances
**Traditional Full Chain:** Service fee 2-6% + FX markup 1-3% = 4-8% typical; banks 12.66% for $200
**Stablecoin Full Chain:** On-ramp 0-4.5% + blockchain <$0.50 + off-ramp 0.5-2.0% + FX 0-0.5% = 1-3% typical; under 1% best case
**Stablecoins win:** High-fee corridors, large txns, liquid off-ramps (Nigeria P2P, Philippines), B2B
**Stablecoins lose:** Small txns ($50-100), thin off-ramp liquidity, unbanked needing cash, strict KYC jurisdictions
**WU USDPT** stablecoin on Solana planned H1 2026; Digital Asset Network
**Scale:** Crypto remittances 3-5% of global flows (~$27-45B); USDT ~$703B/month mostly trading

### 6. Real-Time Payments Impact
